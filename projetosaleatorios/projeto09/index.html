ABOUT

Hi, I'm Rafael Braza, the creator behind 616 Games.
My biggest passion? That gritty, glorious era of '80s and '90s trash
horror — the golden age of VHS tapes.


I’m not just here to make horror games .
I’m here to create unique, terrifying experiences that stick with you
long after the screen fades to black. Right now, I’m focused on the "Tales Beyond the Tomb" series — a
collection of short, standalone horror stories designed to thrill,
unsettle, and surprise.

WITHIN OF STATIC

"Within of Static" is an episodic, first-person psychological horror anthology
inspired by grounded, human fear.
Each episode tells a self-contained story based on those who claim to have
lived through it.
